About

Who I am

I am a technology enthusiast with a strong interest in building products, analyzing data, and understanding the ideas that shape businesses and society. Consumer Sentiment · Case Study

Chennai Express Restaurant

Analyzed 84 public Google reviews for a local restaurant to map sentiment distribution and identify operational risk areas. Classified each review by sentiment and theme — food, service, price, atmosphere, ethics — revealing that service, despite fewer mentions, carried a 71% negative rate, posing the highest reputational risk.

Beyond Work

The rest of me

The things that don't fit a resume but shape how I think.

♟️

Chess

I've been drawn to chess for as long as I can remember — but only since 2022 have I actually invested in learning it properly. GothamChess and Hikaru Nakamura helped me understand what I was actually doing. My favourite player is Mikhail Tal. Not for his results, but for his chaos — he played moves no engine would approve of and made them work through sheer psychological pressure. I find that philosophy interesting beyond the board too.

📚

Books

Adventure and detective fiction. I've read almost all of Tin Goyenda by Rokib Hasan — there's something about a well-constructed mystery that satisfies the same part of the brain as solving a real problem. The tension between what's visible and what's hidden, the moment a pattern clicks. I keep coming back to that.

🏔️

Travel

I've traveled across Bangladesh and made trips to Malaysia, India, Nepal, Bhutan, and Thailand. Mountains are what I keep chasing — there's a stillness at altitude that resets everything. Nepal and Bhutan sit at the top of the list for that reason. Every trip changes how I see things at home.

🎵

Singing

Nothing serious — no stage, no practice schedule. Just occasional jamming sessions when time allows. It's one of the few things I do purely for the feeling of it, with no goal attached. I think everyone needs at least one of those.
